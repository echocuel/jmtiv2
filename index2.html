History

   
  March 1995

JMNet (which later changed name to JM Technology) was established in Osaka (Japan)
  July 1999 JMNet's Fukuoka (Japan) office was established
  March 2000

JMNet's Tokyo (Japan) office was established

  November 2001

JMNet's headquarter was moved from Osaka to Fukuoka (Japan)

  November 2004 JMNet is listed at Fukuoka Stock Exchange's Q-Board (Japan)
  March 2007

JMNet changed name to JM Technology
  March 2009 JM Technology's Tokyo office became Tokyo Headquarter (Japan)
  May 2010 JM Technology's Singapore subsidiary (JM Singapore IT Solutions) was established
  November 2011 JM Technology joined Mamezou OS Holdings group
  March 2012 JM Technology Indonesia started (registered as PT JMT Indonesia)
